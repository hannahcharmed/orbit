What Orbit is built on
The intelligence layer
Most analytics tools give creators numbers. Orbit gives them meaning. Every metric is contextualized, benchmarked, and translated into a clear next action — powered by an AI coaching engine trained on niche-specific creator data.
Gravitational model
Orbit sits at the center of a creator's universe — all platforms, all data, all trends in one gravitational field. Managers and creators see the same truth from different vantage points, enabling faster, smarter decisions together.
Niche-first benchmarking
Comparing a fitness creator to a tech creator is meaningless. Orbit benchmarks against true peers — same niche, similar following size, same platform mix — surfacing insights that generic tools simply cannot produce.
First-party data moat
Built from within a talent agency managing 50+ lifestyle and fitness creators. We launched with real benchmarking data, pre-built creator trust, and a live feedback loop — structural advantages that compound over time.
